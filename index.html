Oatmeal Banana Cookies

Ingredients:

  1. 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  2. 1 cup rolled oats
  3. 1/4 cup almond flour or whole wheat flour
  4. 1/4 cup chopped nuts (such as walnuts or almonds)
  5. 1/4 cup raisins or dried cranberries (optional)
  6. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  8. Pinch of salt

Instructions:

Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a mixing bowl, combine mashed bananas, oats, almond flour (or whole wheat flour), chopped nuts, raisins (if using), vanilla extract, cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Mix well until everything is evenly combined. Using a spoon or your hands, scoop out small portions of the dough and place them onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each portion slightly with your fingers or the back of a spoon. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the cookies are golden brown and firm to the touch. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. These oatmeal banana cookies are naturally sweetened by the bananas and can be customized with your favorite mix-ins like nuts, seeds, or dried fruits. They make for a delicious and healthy snack or dessert option!
